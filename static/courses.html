Course Offerings

First Aid/CPR/AED

The American Red Cross / American Safety and Health Institute (ASHI) training we provide includes a mix of lecture, video, and hands-on practice and assessment. For organizations that don’t have it, we can provide appropriate AV solutions to train your personnel in your own workspace. If you don’t have appropriate facilities, we can work with you to find convenient locations and times to meet your needs. Our goal is to provide maximum flexibility to make sure that everyone who wants and needs this training is able to get it. Our Certifications We can certify participants through either the American Red Cross or the American Safety and Health Institute (ASHI). Which you choose depends on the needs and requirements of your organization. Each organization provides standardized curriculum and supporting media to ensure high-quality training for participants. Contact us to learn the differences between the two programs and which would be best suited for you.
