Who We Are

Hudson Valley Life Skills Trainers is a family-owned business dedicated to delivering the highest quality lifesaving and pediatric care training available. We offer training in Adult, Child and Pediatric CPR/AED/First Aid, Advanced First Aid and Bleeding Control, Wilderness Remote First Aid, and Babysitting Training for adolescents.

Certifications Offered

We provide certifications through the American Red Cross, the American Safety & Health Institute, and Safe Sitter. We will work with you to determine which is right for you. Regardless, our mission is to teach you the skills you need.

Other Services

We can also provide assistance with:

  • Home networking
  • Spreadsheet, Word Processing, Presentation, and Database consultation
  • PC, Tablet, and Phone support
  • Website design
