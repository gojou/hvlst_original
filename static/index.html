Who We Are

Hudson Valley Life Skills Trainers is a family-owned business dedicated to delivering the highest quality lifesaving and pediatric care training available. We offer training in Adult, Child and Pediatric CPR/AED/First Aid, Advanced First Aid and Bleeding Control, Wilderness Remote First Aid, and Babysitting Training for adolescents. We also provide personal technology coaching and consulting.
